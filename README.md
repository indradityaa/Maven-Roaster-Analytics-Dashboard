# Maven Roaster Sales Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze **Maven Roaster sales data** to evaluate overall business performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance across different store locations and time periods. The interactive dashboard helps stakeholders identify sales trends, peak hours, top-selling products, and high-performing store locations to support data-driven business decisions.

## Dataset used
* <a href="https://github.com/indradityaa/Maven-Roaster-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Coffee%20Shop%20Sales.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** Total income generated from all sales transactions.
* **Total Transactions:** Total number of transactions recorded during the sales period.
* **Total Items Sold:** Total quantity of products sold.
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** Average revenue generated per transaction.

### Charts & Analysis

* **Daily Sales Trend:** Visualizes daily changes in revenue and transaction volume.
* **Hourly Sales Trend:** Analyzes sales patterns by hour to identify peak operating hours.
* **Top 5 Products:** Highlights the five best-selling products based on revenue or quantity sold.
* **Sales by Store Location:** Compares sales performance across different store locations.
* **Sales per Category:** Analyzes revenue contribution by product category such as coffee, bakery, and snacks.

## Process

* Reviewed and cleaned the dataset to ensure accuracy and consistency.
* Handled missing values and standardized numerical and categorical fields.
* Created calculated metrics for revenue, AOV, and sales volume.
* Built pivot tables to analyze KPIs across time, product, and store dimensions.
* Designed an interactive Excel dashboard integrating all KPIs and charts.
* Added slicers and filters to allow dynamic exploration of sales performance.

## Project Insights

* Sales trends reveal clear daily and hourly patterns, including peak sales hours.

* Certain store locations consistently outperform others in terms of sales.
* Product category analysis helps identify high-demand product groups.

## Final Conclusion

This Maven Roaster sales analysis dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance across products, time periods, and store locations. By leveraging insights from peak hours, top-selling products, and high-performing branches, the business can optimize staffing, inventory management, and promotional strategies to drive higher revenue and operational efficiency.
